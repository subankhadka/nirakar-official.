About Us

At Nirakr Sarsafai Sewa Pvt. Ltd., our core motive is to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environment for the people of Jhapa and beyond. We are passionately dedicated to delivering professional cleaning and greenery maintenance services that enhance the quality of life in homes, offices, schools, hospitals, industries, and public spaces across the entire Jhapa district.

We believe that cleanliness is not just about appearance—it is deeply connected to health, safety, dignity, and development. By providing reliable and eco-conscious services, we aim to foster a culture of hygiene and environmental care in every community we touch.

Our well-trained and committed team uses modern equipment, scientific techniques, and sustainable practices to ensure that each task is completed with precision and care. At Nirakr, we go beyond basic cleaning—we strive to inspire behavioral change, promote greenery, and contribute to a cleaner, greener, and more beautiful Nepal.
